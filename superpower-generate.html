Good Prompt (CRAFT)

Context: Follow-up email to a cardiologist about CV outcomes data. Role: Act as an MSL communication writer. Action: Rewrite this email to be skimmable: 3 bullets and a one-line ask. Keep it warm, simple, and professional. Under 75 words. Format: Brief greeting, 3 bullet points, one clear ask. Tone: warm, professional, concise.

Missing: context, role, action specifics, format, tone.

Pro tips

Specify the structure.

Ask for bullet counts, headings, and a one-line ask.

List must-include facts.

Tell AI which data points or caveats cannot be dropped.

Keep outputs short.

Give a word limit to avoid overly long drafts.
