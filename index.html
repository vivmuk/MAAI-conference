Explore the 4 superpowers

Each superpower explains how AI delivers the value and includes real Medical Affairs examples.

Generate Ideas

How AI helps: It recombines patterns from similar communications to create fast, diverse options.

  • Email subject lines
  • Meeting talking points
  • Objection responses

Analyze Content

How AI helps: It spots ambiguity, gaps, and compliance risks by scanning structure and tone.

  • Email review for clarity
  • Data gaps analysis
  • Competitor angle stress-test

Generate Content

How AI helps: It drafts structured content quickly while keeping your format requirements.

  • Slide content drafts
